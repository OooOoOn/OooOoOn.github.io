The journey

My professional journey has been a wonderful adventure so far, giving me the opportunity to work in places like London, Barcelona, Malmö and Copenhagen. I harness my creative energy into design, coding and usability, drawing upon my education and previous experience.

Why UX Design

I felt that UX would be a desirable skill to have in addition to development. It would also allow me to take on more challenging and diverse roles.

Recent Experience

I have spent most of 2022 working as a consultant for Adesso Denmark. My role was demanding and fun and I get to work on serveral projects simultaneously as Designer, Product Owner and Developer.

I am currently working on a m-commerce solution for the beauty and health industry. I will be designing the website as well as the mobile application.

I am also taking a course in React.js.

Why Development

I have been coding and scripting since I was a teenager and find it creative and inspiring, especially when I get to work with frontend languages such as HTML and CSS.

What tools do you usually work with?

Mainly Figma, Adobe XD, Balsamiq & MIRO.

Where have you worked with UX?

I did two internships at a consultancy in Copenhagen called Purple Scout / adesso Denmark.

Did you make this website?

Indeed! Front-end programming was included in my program.

Why UX design?

I felt that system development wasn't creative nor sociable enough for my personality. I wanted to work with something in a similar line of work but have a more varying and outgoing role.

Would you be open to freelance work?

Absolutely!
